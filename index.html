ISLAMORADA FISHING CHARTER

Come join me for a fun filled day of inshore fishing in the Florida Keys. Fly fishing or gear fishing, I am able to cater to your needs. The areas fished are the oceanside and back country flats of ISLAMORADA, KEY LARGO, and the EVERGLADES NATIONAL PARK.

Species will include: Tarpon, Bonefish, Permit, Redfish, Snook, Sea Trout, Snappers and Sharks

Perfect for Learning or Expiranced Fishers!


The Florida Keys are famous for world class fishing. Whether fishing for snappers in the mangroves or poling the edge of a flat looking for tarpon, or in the shallow water hunting bonefish, Days will be set up to provide anglers with the best opportunity, taking into consideration the current conditions, the anglers desire, and their skill level.

Trips

Please call at 305.393.0663

The Boat I operate out of is a 2019 17 Maverick HPXV, that can accomodate me and two anglers. A third angler can be added if they are a small child. (I must consider future generations of clients afterall!) Fishing gear is provided, but if you have a favorite fly rod or spin rod please feel free to bring it along.

Polarized sun glasses, a hat, sunscreen, and a rain jacket are reccomended. Please also bring any food or drinks (besides water) that you would like for the day. I will have a cooler on board with ice and bottled water.

Most of my trips leave out of Angler House Marina in Islamorada. Located at mile marker 80.5 on US 1 South. Angler House Marina
